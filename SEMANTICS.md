**6. "Canonical" has two strengths.** Strong-canonical = forced (no alternative, G.1). Weak-canonical = naturally preferred (Dist-ward functor is natural, Co-Dist-ward is not). Specify which.

**7. The three meta-primitives track the three projections.** Every structural act named by the framework's vocabulary decomposes into Observer Act (P3), Productive Act (P1), and Mediating Act (P2). The decomposition is exhaustive (SEM-1).
